EOZ &ETH

ABOUT EOZ

EOZ is a cryptocurrency for services performed in the Thoedoz ecosystem. It is a friendly fork of Ethereum at a block height of #7,080,000, as Ethereum is switching to PoS and mining will end soon. We empower miners by continuing to support the PoW consensus. Everyone can mine EOZ and earn rewards globally, and this is true decentralization of wealth.
‍
Ethereum holders as of block 7,080,000 will receive EOZ. Join us to democratize finance and change the world with tokenized economy.

EOZ &ETH

EZ LEND

An open peer-to-peer marketplace for obtaining a loan on your own terms by placing EOZ or physical commodities as collateral. With supported non-fungible ERC1400 token standard, receiving a loan has never been easier.
